How to play Scout's Duel
  • Both players secretly pick one footballer from a shared pool of 24 cards.
  • Take turns asking yes/no questions to narrow down the opponent's pick.
  • Click cards to flip & eliminate them after each answer.
  • Press 🎯 Shoot to make a direct guess — correct wins, wrong ends your turn.
  • No timer pressure. Take your time and make smarter deductions.
